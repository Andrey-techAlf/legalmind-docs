Privacy Policy for Legal Mind

Effective date: December 1, 2025

Legal Mind ("we", "our", "the App") provides AI-powered document analysis and legal chat assistance. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

We collect a randomly generated device identifier for the following purposes:

  • managing free-tier usage limits,
  • preventing abuse.

This identifier does not include your name, Apple ID, email, phone number, or any personal information.

1.2 Documents & Images

The documents and images you scan or upload are used only for generating analysis results.

  • Files are stored temporarily on your device.
  • They are transmitted securely to our AI provider (Gemini API by Google).
  • They are never stored permanently on our servers.
  • They are deleted automatically after processing.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage data to improve app performance, such as:

  • number of document analyses,
  • feature usage,
  • error reporting.

No personal identifiers are included in analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • provide AI document analysis,
  • ensure service functionality and performance,
  • enforce fair usage limits,
  • improve features and stability.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Temporary files use iOS Data Protection where available.
  • All communication with our server and Gemini API uses HTTPS encryption.
  • We do not store your documents after analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party provider:

Google Gemini API

  • We send text and images to Gemini for AI analysis.
  • Files and messages are processed only to generate output.
  • No personal profiles are created.

For more details, see Google's privacy documentation.

5. Children's Privacy

Legal Mind is not intended for users under 16 years of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will always be available on this page.
